Noch Caffè
Visual Identity

About the project

Noch Caffè is a café located in the province of Vicenza, Italy, offering a wide selection of high-quality food and beverages. Its identity is built around floral illustrations sourced from an encyclopedic collection, giving the brand a timeless and refined look. In addition to the visual identity, I also created social media content for the café, including photography and video production.
